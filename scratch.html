Finance

 

 

Finance is a term for matters regarding the management, creation, and study of money and investments. It involves the use of credit and debt, securities, and investment to finance current projects using future income flows. Because of this temporal aspect, finance is closely linked to the time value of money, interest rates, and other related topics.(https://www.investopedia.com/terms/f/finance.asp)


Within Finance there are many career's that one can pursue but mainly 5:

  1. Coprate Finance & Advisory
      -Capital budgeting
      -Capital structure
      -Working capital management
  2. Investment Banking
  3. Private Equity & Venture Capital
  4. Real Estate
  5. Asset Management
